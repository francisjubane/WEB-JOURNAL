What is Big Data?

  • Big Data’ is similar to ‘ small data’, but bigger in size.
  • But having data bigger requires different approaches:
    -Techniques, tools, and architecture
  • An aim to solve new problems or old problems in a better way.
  • Big Data generates value from the storage processing of very large quantities of digital information that cannot be analyzed with tradition computing techniques.
